All about Otters!

Learn fun and fascinating facts about otters!

Otter Gallery 1

Otters are very cute and unique creatures that can live in both fresh and salt water. These animals are carnivorous and tend to eat many aquatic creatures such as frogs, fish, turtles, crayfish, and other small mammals. However, even if their plate may seem rather intimidating, they're actually quite lovable, as some even hold hands to help each other from drifting away while sleeping! Other times, they'll cover their eyes while they sleep so as to not be bothered by the sunlight.

Otter Gallery 2

Otters are one of the cutest animals in my opinion. They manage to be fun and friendly when domesticated, often playing with basketballs or other spherical objects in an attempt to exert their energy. Often, otter owners will let otters break ice to replicate the feeling of cracking clams or oysters with a rock while in the wild.
